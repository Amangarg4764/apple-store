Product Discription

Beautiful Design

A brilliant 11.94-cm (4.7) Retina HD display and the speed and security of Touch ID provide you a truly enhanced user experience. The iPhone SE, that comes in a slim and light design, is water resistant for up to 1 metre for about 30 minutes.

Performance - Experience the Speed of A13 Bionic

With the incredibly fast A13 Bionic chip in this smartphone, you get to launch apps, play games and more - all with blazing speed.

Portrait Mode - All Attention Is on You

With a single-lens camera and machine learning technology, your pictures will be captured with you being in focus. The Bokeh Effect adds a sophisticated background blur to your image. What's more? You can also adjust the depth of field on your portrait images after you have shot them.

Smart HDR - A Picture with Good Highlight

With the presence of new secondary frames, faster sensors, and the powerful A12 Bionic chip, your HDR images, taken from the rear camera, will be enhanced with more highlight and shadow.

High Definition Video

With 4K video, get ready to experience four times the detail of Full HD. Also, with QuickTake, you can start recording video in photo mode by just holding the shutter.

Binge-worthy Battery Life

Providing up to 13 hours of video watching on a single charge, the iPhone SE keeps the show going on.
